Caroline Pickens Senior Content Marketing Manager Caroline Pickens is the Senior Content Marketing Manager at EDB. Her focus is on using great storytelling to create content that educates future and current customers on the benefits of Postgres, the value of working with EDB and its strength in handling modern data challenges. Before joining EDB in December 2023, she was the senior writer on Asurion’s Content Marketing team. A veteran of the TV news business, she honed her copywriting and storytelling skills in the hectic newsrooms of broadcast journalism. Caroline holds a bachelor’s degree in Journalism from Elon University in North Carolina. Caroline lives in Nashville, Tennessee and enjoys traveling, cooking, gardening, and spending time outdoors.
