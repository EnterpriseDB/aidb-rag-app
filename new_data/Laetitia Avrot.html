Lætitia Avrot Field CTO, France, EDB Lætitia Avrot is Field CTO for EDB in France. She began working with PostgreSQL in 2007. Previously, she learned Oracle and SQL Server at the same time. She has had a lot of exciting experiences with high availability, production crashes, disaster recovery plans, load balancing and spatial data. She’s involved with the community as she was elected PostgreSQL Europe treasurer, co-founded Postgres Women and is a recognized PostgreSQL project contributor.
